Be a witness to an inspiring work culture. Join us!

Interested applicants can apply through [Careers@Gov](http://www.careers.gov.sg/){:target="_blank"}, the one-stop job search portal for the Public Service. Alternatively you can complete and submit the [Public Service Application Form(PDF, 127KB)](/files/PSAF.pdf/) to our <a href="mailto:recruitment@mlaw.gov.sg">Human Resource Department</a>. To view current available positions, click [here](http://careers.pageuppeople.com/688/cwlive/en/filter/?=&search-keyword=ministry%20of%20law&brand=ministry%20of%20law&job-mail-subscribe-privacy=agree){:target="_blank"}.

Only shortlisted candidates will be notified.
